Who we are

Welcome to Coleflax Bennett Architecture, from inception to completion, our client centric approach fosters exceptional quality, value and lasting partnerships

Welcome to Coleflax Bennett Architecture. With over 40 years of combined experience, our team has been delivering both residential and commercial projects through to completion.

We provide full architectural services from RIBA Stage 1 to Stage 7 — from initial concept and planning through to technical design and project delivery.

We pride ourselves on continuity, forming a personal relationship with our clients to properly understand their ideas, space and how design can add functionality to their lives. Our process is built on clear communication, collaboration and creative thinking.

And we can’t wait to hear your ideas - Jonathan & Matthew.
