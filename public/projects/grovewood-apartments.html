Grovewood Apartments, Bessborough, Harrow, HA1

Project Details

Location

Bessborough, Harrow, HA1

Project Value

£5.5m

Involvement

Post Planning Riba stage 3 - 6

Redevelopment of synagogue site to Bessborough Road to develop approved planning design by others and provide Full technical design and delivery of project on site.

The Project involved the demolition of the existing synagogue building on site to be replaced by a 6 storey residential development comprising 40 apartments with a mix of 1 & 2 bed apartments
