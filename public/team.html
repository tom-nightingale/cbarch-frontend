I have been working the Architectural profession for over 20 years having worked at some of Nottinghamshire’s largest Architectural Practices. Working across a range of sectors including private residential, student living, and retail. I established my own Architectural practice Coleflax Design back in 2016. Following successful growth Coleflax Bennett Architecture was formed in 2019 with Matthew.

I passionately believe in fostering good relationships with our clients, delivering work which not just meets expectations but exceeds them time and again. Our continued repeat business with our valued clients is testament to the quality of work that we can deliver and the trust that is placed in us by them.

I fundamentally believe we shouldn’t compromise on the quality of our Architecture no matter what type and scale of building is being considered for our clients.
